Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a project?
The timeline depends on the complexity and scope of your project. Generally, CV/resume services take 2-3 days, while projects may take 2-4 weeks. We provide a specific timeline after reviewing your requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept mobile money payments through MTN Mobile Money and Orange Money. Payment is required before we begin work on your project.
Can I request revisions?
Yes, we offer up to 3 rounds of revisions for all our services to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product.
Do you guarantee good grades?
While we provide high-quality work following academic standards and guidelines, final grades depend on multiple factors including your institution's specific requirements and your defense performance.
Is my personal information kept confidential?
Absolutely. We have a strict privacy policy and never share your personal information or project details with third parties.
